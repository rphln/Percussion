    animated_emoji_to_id(text)

    View Source 
    animated_emoji_to_id(String.t()) :: {:ok, Nostrum.Snowflake.t()} | :error

    Converts a formatted Discord animated emoji to a Nostrum.Snowflake.t/0.

    Examples 

    iex> Percussion.Converters.animated_emoji_to_id("<a:thonk:123456789>")
{:ok, 123456789}

iex> Percussion.Converters.animated_emoji_to_id("<:thonk:123456789>")
:error

iex> Percussion.Converters.animated_emoji_to_id("<@123456789>")
:error

iex> Percussion.Converters.animated_emoji_to_id("123456789")
:error
